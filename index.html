What is Bharat.io?

Bharat.io is designed for modern cities where privacy, safety and quick communication matter. Instead of writing phone numbers on vehicles, owners place a unique QR sticker that anyone can scan to reach them during genuine parking or emergency situations.

Vehicle owners register their contact details once. After registration, the QR works automatically — no apps, no logins, no tracking, and no misuse for marketing or spam.

How It Works

A simple 3-step process to stay reachable while protecting privacy.

1

Register Once

Register your vehicle number, mobile number, and optional business profile on Bharat.io. Takes under 2 minutes.

2

Paste QR Sticker

A unique QR sticker is placed on your vehicle. No personal information is visible publicly on the sticker.

3

Scan & Connect

Anyone facing a parking issue or emergency scans the QR and connects with you instantly — safely and privately.
