About the System

What is Link For You?

Link For You is designed for modern cities where privacy, safety and quick communication matter. Instead of writing phone numbers on vehicles, owners place a unique QR sticker that anyone can scan to reach them during genuine parking or emergency situations.

Vehicle owners voluntarily register their contact details once. After registration, the QR works automatically — no apps, no logins, no tracking, and no misuse for marketing or spam.

Simple Process

How It Works

A simple 3-step process to stay reachable while protecting privacy.

1

Register Once

The vehicle owner registers their vehicle number, mobile number, and optional business profile details on Link For You.

2

Paste QR Sticker

A unique QR sticker is placed on the vehicle. No personal information is visible publicly.

3

Scan & Connect

Anyone facing a parking issue or emergency scans the QR and connects with the vehicle owner instantly through the website.
