About the System

What is Link For You?

Link For You is designed for modern cities where privacy, safety and quick communication matter. Instead of writing phone numbers on vehicles, owners place a unique QR sticker that anyone can scan to reach them during genuine parking or emergency situations.

Vehicle owners voluntarily register their contact details once. After registration, the QR works automatically — no apps, no logins, no tracking, and no misuse for marketing or spam.
