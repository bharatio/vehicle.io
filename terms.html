1. About This Platform

Link For You is a QR based vehicle contact platform developed and operated by Canacot Systems. The platform allows vehicle owners to register their contact details so that other people can reach them during genuine parking conflicts or emergency situations by scanning a QR sticker placed on the vehicle.

Link For You is a technology intermediary only. The platform does not participate in any communication that takes place between users after contact is made.

3. Phone Number Display on Dialer

When a visitor taps the Call button on a vehicle contact profile, the registered mobile number is passed directly to the visitor's device dialer using a standard telephone link. This is a technical function of the visitor's own device and operating system.

Link For You does not record, intercept, route or store any phone call. The platform only provides the contact link. Any call that takes place is entirely between the two individuals involved.
